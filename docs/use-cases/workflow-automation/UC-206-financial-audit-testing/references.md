## Source Quality Notes

The evidence base for this use case is anchored by Big Four deployment announcements (S1, S2, S4, S7, S8) that confirm market direction and investment scale, but lack the independent ROI quantification found in other enterprise AI use cases (e.g., the Forrester TEI studies available for tax compliance platforms). The KPMG 2025 SOX Survey (S5) is the strongest source for baseline cost and effort data — it is a structured survey with specific metrics across multiple organizations. MindBridge (S3) provides the clearest product evidence for AI-powered audit analytics with published platform-scale metrics (260B+ transactions, 3,000+ ERPs), though performance claims (false positive reduction, detection rates) are vendor-published without independent verification. The PCAOB TIA deliverable (S6) is authoritative regulatory guidance but is directional — final standards for AI in auditing are not yet published. The IIA Global Internal Audit Standards (S10) are authoritative for internal audit methodology. The Genpact-MindBridge partnership announcement (S9) is a vendor press release useful for integration capabilities but not for independent performance evidence. Efficiency claims (40% from Deloitte, 80% from RSM) cited in evaluation economics are vendor or industry-reported figures, not independently measured in controlled studies.
